Club Captains

 

Submit Your Monthly Report
The Club Captain should complete a report at the end of each month detailing what the Club did and accomplished. This allows Mozilla to celebrate your successes, connect you with opportunities, and know your Club is still active!

Regional Coordinators

Submit a Monthly Report
A Regional Coordinator should send a report to Mozilla Campus staff in a monthly base updating us on your progress towards your goals. This ensures you will always have the necessary support and resources for supporting your Campus Clubs.
